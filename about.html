About Tafiti Africa

Founded in 2023, Tafiti Africa is a pan-African technology company dedicated to empowering businesses across the continent through innovative data analytics and technology solutions.

Our name "Tafiti" comes from the Swahili word meaning "to research" or "to discover", reflecting our mission to help businesses uncover valuable insights from their data and discover new opportunities through technology.

With a team of passionate African technologists, data scientists, and business experts, we combine global best practices with deep local knowledge to deliver solutions that truly work in the African context.
